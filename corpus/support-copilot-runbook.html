Support Copilot Runbook

When a support copilot answers with weak evidence, the first response is to inspect the retrieved chunks, overlap terms, and rerank margin before changing prompts.

The platform should treat missing citations and low-support answers as retrieval incidents, not as prompt-tuning problems. Operations teams should confirm whether the source article was ingested, whether the chunk boundary hid the relevant sentence, and whether the dense embedding signal disagreed with the sparse keyword signal.
